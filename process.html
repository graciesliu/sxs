Sight × Site

About

We are a team of art, design, architecture, and engineering students in collaboration with WashU Medical School’s Barnes-Jewish Hospital Ophthalmology Clinic to develop and produce multi-sensory sited experiences that aim to engage people empathically with stories from the visually impaired community, exploring the intersection of health and design through narrative medicine.
